UPS Shipping Restriction

Please do not enter a P.O. Box for your shipping address. All orders ship via UPS, and UPS will not deliver to P.O. Boxes.

  • Free Shipping: All orders with a subtotal of $50 or more and shipping within the contiguous 48 U.S. states will get UPS Ground shipping at no charge. All other shipping methods, including expedited, will receive our discounted UPS rates.
  • Expedited Processing: If selecting an expedited shipping method, please be aware that there will still be a 2-3 business day processing period before we ship your order. If your order is received before 10a CST, it will be entered that day and the processing time starts there. If the order is received after 10a CST, it will be entered the next business day and the processing time will begin there.

Freight Shipping Procedure

  • If your total shipping weight is over 150 pounds, please select the NPS - Over 150# Weight Shipping option. This will pass $0 shipping on your order and Customer Service will recalculate freight shipping and email you the cost. We will then apply that cost to your order and payment method.
