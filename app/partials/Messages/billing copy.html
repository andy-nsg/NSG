Payment Information

Credit Cards

Charges on credit card statements will appear as Industries for the Blind and Visually Impaired, Inc. We accept prepayment by credit card: VISA and MasterCard. Please be aware that your credit card will be charged at the moment you submit your order (the transaction will be authorized and settled right away). If you have any questions regarding this or need to make other arrangements, please notify us right away and we'll work with you to fit your organization's needs.

Purchase Orders

We gladly accept Purchase Orders from established organizations. To set your organization up on Net/Credit Terms with us, please complete our Credit Application and email it to service@nationalservicegear.org. The process typically takes 2-10 business days.

Check Payments

To pay by check, please contact us at service@nationalservicegear.org to get set up. Please note that orders paid by check will not ship until we have received and processed your payment.

Sales Tax

Because Industries for the Blind and Visually Impaired, Inc. has a physical or economic presence ("Nexus") in the following states, we are required by law to collect sales tax:

  • AZ, CA, CO, CT, DC, FL, GA, IL, LA, MD, MA, MN, MS, NV, NM, OH, PA, SC, TN, TX, UT, VT, and WI.

Tax-Exempt Organizations: Please email your State Tax Exempt Certificate to service@nationalservicegear.org before placing your order to ensure tax is not applied.

Government Tax-Exempt Status: This storefront is configured specifically for tax-exempt government transactions.

If you are using a Government Purchase Card (GPC) or official funds, tax will not be applied to this order. If you see tax appearing in your cart in error, please contact us at 1.855.444.0847 before submitting your order.
