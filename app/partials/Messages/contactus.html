Who We Are

National Service Gear is a brand of Industries for the Blind and Visually Impaired, Inc (IBVI). Our mission is to provide and grow meaningful employment opportunities for people who are blind or visually impaired. IBVI is an AbilityOne, non-profit organization dedicated to providing employment opportunities to blind professionals through the identification and fulfillment of customer needs. We serve a wide range of customers from the federal government and military installations, to Fortune 500 companies and small businesses. We provide these companies with the supplies they need to keep daily operations running smoothly. These supplies include anything and everything from office supplies to furniture and janitorial to tool and maintenance products. Learn more about IBVI.

Payment information

Credit Cards

Charges on credit card statements will appear as Industries for the Blind and Visually Impaired, Inc. We accept prepayment by credit card - VISA, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover are all accepted on the webstore. Please be aware that if you are paying by credit card, your order will be authorized and settled at the moment you submit your order. If you have any questions regarding this or need to make other arrangements, please notify us right away and we'll work with you to fit your organization's needs.

Purchase Orders

Purchase Orders are accepted and we just need to get your organization setup with ours on credit terms first. To do this, we just need you to fill out our Credit Application and email it back to us at service@nationalservicegear.org. The whole process takes about 2-10 business days depending on your trade references and how quickly they get back to us. Any questions or concerns, just give us a holler.

Check Payments

If you want to be setup to prepay by check, no problem, please contact us via email at service@nationalservicegear.org. Check orders won't ship until after we've received your check.

Sales Tax

Industries for the Blind and Visually Impaired, Inc. does have a footprint in certain states and must charge state sales tax for any orders being shipped to one of these states: Arizona, California, District of Columbia, Florida, Illinois, Louisiana, Maryland, Massachusetts, Minnesota, Mississippi, Nevada, New Mexico, Ohio, Pennsylvania, South Carolina, Texas, Utah, and Wisconsin.

If your organization is exempt from state sales taxes in the state we're shipping your order to, please email a copy of your State Tax Exempt Certificate to Customer Service at service@nationalservicegear.org or fax it to 1.414.778.3041 before placing your order. We appreciate your help in this matter as it keeps us from paying additional fees to reprocess credit card payments.

Shipping and Processing

All orders will be processed in 2-3 business days. If your order is received before 10a CST, it will be entered that day and the process starts there. If the order is received after 10a CST, it will be entered the next business day and the process will begin there.

Any orders with expedited shipping will ship out via that method after processing. If the order is received after 10a CST, processing will begin the next business days. Same day processing available for stock items only by request with Customer Service - same day order processing must be requested before 10a CST by calling into Customer Service at 1.855.444.0847.

Orders originate from Southern Wisconsin. Click here to view a shipping map.

All packages ship UPS and cannot be delivered to a PO Box. If other arrangements need to be made with a different shipping method, please call Customer Service at 1.855.444.0847.

All orders shipping UPS Ground within the contiguous 48 U.S. states is free. All other shipping methods, including expedited, will receive discounted UPS rates.
