Our Mission & Who We Are

National Service Gear is a brand of Industries for the Blind and Visually Impaired, Inc. (IBVI). We are a proud AbilityOne® not-for-profit organization and an ISO 9001:2005 certified company.

At IBVI, our marching orders are straightforward and serve as the foundation for everything we do:

  • Create and grow employment opportunities for people who are blind or visually impaired.
  • Provide the best, most cost-effective industrial supplies and friendly customer service.
  • Be the leading voice in educating businesses on how to create, design, and build accessible workplaces for the blind and visually impaired.
  • Balance job creation, capital investments, and profitability while adhering to regulatory requirements.
  • Create an open, collaborative environment that fosters innovative business ideas, engages employees, and adds a true sense of belonging.

By partnering with us, you are directly supporting upward mobility for the blind and visually impaired and helping us bring new purpose to the lives of our team members.

A Note on AmeriCorps Inventory

Due to the evolving landscape of the AmeriCorps program, we are transitioning many items that were previously "Stock" into a Made-To-Order (MTO) model. This ensures a wider variety of gear remains available for your programs year-round.

National Park Service Inventory

Items in this store are a combination of Made-To-Order (MTO) gear and Customer-Owned Inventory. Government-owned stock is managed specifically for NPS parks and centers to ensure consistent availability for your personnel.

Made-To-Order Program

To provide you with the greatest selection of garments and customization options, this store operates on a Made-To-Order (MTO) model. We do not maintain a pre-printed "shelf" stock; instead, each item is crafted specifically for your order.

Shipping & Processing

General Processing: All orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Orders received before 11 AM ET are entered the same day.

Stock Items: Items currently in stock ship within 2-3 business days. If unavailable, they will be placed on backorder.

Production Timeline: MTO and Custom items ship based on the lead time listed in the product description.

Branding Techniques

We use industry-leading equipment to ensure your gear looks professional and lasts:

  • Embroidery: High-density stitching for a premium, 3D finish on polos and hats.
  • Screen Printing: The gold standard for large bulk orders.
  • DTF (Direct to Film): Ideal for complex, full-color logos with a soft-feel finish.

Custom & Made-To-Order (MTO) Workflow

  1. Order Placement: Place your order via the webstore.
  2. Digital Proofing: We send a design proof via Adobe Acrobat Request Signature (New orders only).
  3. Approval: Once art is approved, the production lead time begins.
  4. Shipment: Your gear is finished, inspected, and shipped.

Reorders of existing art skip the proofing process to speed up production.

Payment, Tax & Returns

Payment: We accept VISA and MasterCard. Established organizations may apply for Net Terms.

Sales Tax: We collect tax in 22 states where IBVI has a presence. Tax-exempt organizations should email certificates to service@nationalservicegear.org before ordering.

Returns: Stock items are subject to a 10% restocking fee. Custom/MTO items are non-returnable unless defective.
