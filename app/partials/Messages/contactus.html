Our Mission & Who We Are

National Service Gear is a brand of Industries for the Blind and Visually Impaired, Inc. (IBVI). We are a proud AbilityOne® not-for-profit organization and an ISO 9001 certified company.

At IBVI, our marching orders serve as the foundation for everything we do:

  • Create and grow employment opportunities for people who are blind or visually impaired.
  • Provide the best, most cost-effective industrial supplies and friendly customer service.
  • Be the leading voice in educating businesses on how to create accessible workplaces.
  • Balance job creation and capital investments while adhering to regulatory requirements.
  • Create an open, collaborative environment that fosters innovative business ideas.

By partnering with us, you are directly supporting upward mobility for the blind and visually impaired and helping us bring new purpose to the lives of our team members.

A Note on AmeriCorps Inventory

Due to the evolving landscape of the AmeriCorps program, we are transitioning many items that were previously "Stock" into a Made-To-Order (MTO) model.

National Park Service Inventory

Items in this store are a combination of Made-To-Order (MTO) gear and Customer-Owned Inventory managed specifically for NPS parks.

Made-To-Order Program

This store operates on an MTO model. We do not maintain a pre-printed "shelf" stock; items are crafted specifically for your order.

Shipping, Processing & Branding

General Processing: Orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Orders received before 11 AM ET are entered the same day.

Stock Items: In-stock items ship within 2-3 business days. Backordered items ship as they become available.

Production Timeline: MTO and Custom items ship based on the lead time listed in the product description.

We utilize Embroidery for premium finishes, Screen Printing for high-volume orders, and DTF (Direct to Film) for complex, full-color designs.

Payment & Sales Tax

Payment Methods: We accept VISA and MasterCard. Organizations may apply for Net Terms or contact us for Check Prepayment setup.

Tax-Exempt? Email your certificate to service@nationalservicegear.org before ordering.

Returns & Defective Items

Inspection: Please inspect items immediately. Discrepancies must be reported within 5 business days of receipt.

Stock Returns: Stock items must be returned within 30 days in unworn condition. A 10% restocking fee applies.

Custom/MTO Items: These items cannot be returned except for manufacturer defects. If you receive a defective item, notify us within 5 business days at service@nationalservicegear.org for a Return Authorization.
