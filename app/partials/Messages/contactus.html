Our Mission & Who We Are

National Service Gear is a brand of Industries for the Blind and Visually Impaired, Inc. (IBVI). We are a proud AbilityOne® not-for-profit organization and an ISO 9001 certified company.

At IBVI, our marching orders serve as the foundation for everything we do:

  • Create and grow employment opportunities for people who are blind or visually impaired.
  • Provide the best, most cost-effective industrial supplies and friendly customer service.
  • Be the leading voice in educating businesses on how to create, design, and build accessible workplaces for the blind and visually impaired.
  • Balance job creation, capital investments, and profitability while adhering to regulatory requirements.
  • Create an open, collaborative environment that fosters innovative business ideas, engages employees, and adds a true sense of belonging.

By partnering with us, you are directly supporting upward mobility for the blind and visually impaired and helping us bring new purpose to the lives of our team members.

Shipping & Processing

General Processing: All orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Orders received before 11 AM ET are entered the same day.

Stock Items: In-stock items ship within 2-3 business days. If unavailable, they will be placed on backorder.

Production Timeline: MTO and Custom items ship based on the lead time listed in the product description.

Branding Techniques

We use industry-leading equipment to ensure your custom gear looks professional and lasts. Depending on your garment and logo, we utilize the following methods:

  • Embroidery: Best for a premium, professional look on polos, hats, and outerwear. This uses high-density stitching for a durable, 3D finish.
  • Screen Printing: The gold standard for large bulk orders of t-shirts and hoodies. Best for designs with 1-3 solid colors.
  • DTF (Direct to Film): Ideal for complex, full-color logos and smaller quantities. DTF provides a vibrant, soft-feel finish that is highly durable and won't crack or peel.

Custom & Made-To-Order (MTO) Workflow

  1. Order Placement: Place your order via the webstore.
  2. Digital Proofing: We send a design proof via Adobe Acrobat Request Signature (New orders only).
  3. Approval: Once art is approved, the production lead time begins.
  4. Shipment: Your gear is finished, inspected, and shipped.

Reorders of existing art skip the proofing process to speed up production.

Returns & Defective Items

Inspection: Please count and inspect items immediately. Discrepancies must be reported within 5 business days of receipt.

Stock Returns: Stock items must be returned within 30 days in unworn condition. A 10% restocking fee applies.
