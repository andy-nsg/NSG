Who We Are

Thank you for shopping with National Service Gear. National Service Gear is the official provider of volunteer clothing, gear, and recruitment items to the Corporation for National & Community Service and its programs. If you don't see what you're looking for, please contact Customer Service at 1.855.444.0847 or email us at service@nationalservicegear.org. We'll work with you to find a custom product that meets the needs of your program and targeted audience!

National Service Gear is a brand of Industries for the Blind, Inc. Our mission is to provide and grow meaningful employment opportunities for people who are blind or visually impaired. Industries for the Blind, Inc. is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing employment opportunities to blind professionals through the identification and fulfillment of customer needs. We serve a wide range of customers from the federal government and military installations, to Fortune 500 companies and small businesses. We provide these companies with the supplies they need to keep daily operations running smoothly. These supplies include anything and everything from office supplies to furniture and janitorial to tool and maintenance products. Learn more about IB.

Payment information

Credit Cards

Charges on credit card statements will appear as Industries for the Blind, Inc. We accept prepayment by credit card - VISA, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover are all accepted on the webstore. Please be aware that if you are paying by credit card, your order will be authorized and settled at the moment you submit your order. If you have any questions regarding this or need to make other arrangements, please notify us right away and we'll work with you to fit your organization's needs.

Purchase Orders

Purchase Orders are accepted however we need to get your organization setup with ours on credit terms. To do this, we just need you to fill out our Credit Application and email it back to us at service@nationalservicegear.org. The whole process takes about 2-10 business days depending on your trade references and how quickly they get back to us. Any questions or concerns, just give us a holler.

Check Payments

If you want to be setup to prepay by check, no problem, please contact us via email at service@nationalservicegear.org. Check orders won't ship until after we've received your check.

Sales Tax

Industries for the Blind, Inc. does have a footprint in certain states and must charge state sales tax for any orders being shipped to one of these states: California, Connecticut, District of Columbia, Florida, Illinois, Maryland, Massachusetts, Nebraska, Nevada, New Mexico, Ohio, Pennsylvania, South Carolina, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, Virginia, Washington, and Wisconsin.

If your organization is exempt from state sales taxes in the state we're shipping your order to, please email a copy of your State Tax Exempt Certificate to Customer Service at service@nationalservicegear.org or fax it to 608-836-1640 before placing your order. We appreciate your help in this matter as it keeps us from paying additional fees to reprocess credit card payments.

Shipping and Processing

All orders will be processed in 2-3 business days. If your order is received before 10a CST, it will be entered that day and the process starts there. If the order is received after 10a CST, it will be entered the next business day and the process will begin there.

Any orders with expedited shipping will ship out via that method after processing. If the order is received after 10a CST, processing will begin the next business days. Same day processing available for stock items only by request with Customer Service - same day order processing must be requested before 10a CST by calling into Customer Service at 1.855.444.0847.

Orders originate from Southern Wisconsin. Click here to view a shipping map.

All packages ship UPS and cannot be delivered to a PO Box. If other arrangements need to be made with a different shipping method, please call Customer Service at 1.855.444.0847.

For free UPS Ground shipping on orders over $300, please enter the Promotion Code SHIP300 at checkout.

Any free shipping promotions apply only to UPS Ground Shipping within the contiguous United States. If the promo is entered and expedited shipping is selected, the promo will be removed upon receipt of order by Customer Service. If an order is made for a location outside the contiguous United States, Customer Service will apply the UPS Ground Rate and email the customer who made the order. For a shipping quote for expedited shipping or rates outside the contiguous United States, please call Customer Service at 1.855.444.0847.

Placing An Order

To Order Stock Merchandise

Select products featured in the store are carried in Stock, pre-printed with the AmeriCorps logo and available immediately. These items are identified by the item number prefix 'C-'. To order Stock merchandise, simply pick your items and quantities, add to your shopping cart and proceed through the checkout process.

To Order Purchase Power Items

To offer a wide variety of gear and colors, we are very proud to offer our Purchase Power program. Purchase Power items run on a monthly cycle and are shipped out based off of the schedule provided under the respective item's description. These items are identified by the item number prefix 'P-'. To order Purchase Power merchandise, simply pick your items and quantities, add to your shopping cart and proceed through the checkout process. If you choose an expedited shipping method for this item, it will ship via that method after the scheduled production time.

To Order Digitally Printed Items

We love our digital printer. We can print just about anything on most tees and hoods at a low cost and pass those savings to you. Anything printed with our digital will typically ship out in 10-15 business days, unless noted differently under the respective item's description. These items are identified by the item number prefix 'KD-'. To order Digital merchandise, simply pick your items and quantities, add to your shopping cart and proceed through the checkout process. If you choose an expedited shipping method for this item, it will ship via that method after the scheduled production time.

To Order Custom Products

Are you looking for items that feature your organization or program name? We offer a wide variety of items with the option to add your own customized designs! We have many items that can be customized or branded to fit your needs. Some of our items have the option built in to brand or customize. If you see any item that you would like branded or customized that doesn't have that option, please contact us and we'll help you get your order placed. Custom orders typically ship out after 10-20 business days.

To Obtain Quote or Pre-Order Invoice

We understand that you may need a written quote or pre-order invoice prior to ordering. If this is the case, please shoot us an email with the items you need and all pertinent information and we'll reply back with your required docs.
